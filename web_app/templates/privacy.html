Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Music Theory Engine ("we", "us", "our") provides an online music theory analysis and composition tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent
  • Music Data: Compositions, chord progressions, and scales you create or analyze
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for session management

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our music theory services
  • Analyze chord progressions and generate MIDI files
  • Display interactive fretboard and virtual keyboard content
  • Understand usage patterns to improve user experience

4. Data Storage

Your compositions and settings are stored locally in your browser (localStorage). We do not store your personal compositions on our servers unless you explicitly save them for sharing.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Render: Hosting infrastructure
  • Google Analytics: Usage analytics
  • music21/mingus: Music theory processing (runs locally)

6. Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may clear your browser localStorage at any time to remove locally stored data. For GDPR/CCPA rights, contact us at the email below.
